About

I create web applications that provide users with amazing online experiences.

My primary focus is on responsiveness, accessibility, and performance optimization, and how those things can help to serve the greatest number of users possible.

I have also given several presentations at my local developer meetup on topics such as:

  • What front-end developers need to know about Cross Origin Resource Sharing
  • How to use WordPress as a headless CMS with React
  • Using spaced repetition systems to learn more effectively

Talking to — and learning from — other people who are passionate about the web is one of my greatest joys.

When I'm not in front of my computer monitor or engaging with other developers, I enjoy cycling and eating copious amounts of pizza.
