About

After studying music and audio technology in college Eric went on to teach English in Japan for two years.

When he returned to the USA Eric continued to serve others while working for the Maryland Judiciary. Though he enjoyed providing a valuable service to the public in his, he found himself longing for greater intellectual challenges and an outlet for his creative thinking and problem solving abilities. So he start learning to code in late 2018 and has been obsessed ever since.

Now Eric spends as much time as possible learning everything he can about web technologies with a heavy focus on HTML, CSS, and vanilla Javascript. He is currently seeking a role as a junior front end web developer.
